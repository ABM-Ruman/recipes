Ingredients:
 - 1 cups hot water
 - teabag
 - sugar as required
 - milk as required

Directions:
 1. Take some hot water in a cup.

 2. Put teabag in it. In a large bowl, combine the sugar, flour, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda
    and salt.  Add eggs, milk, oil and vanilla. Mix until smooth.  Stir in
    boiling water (batter will be thin). Pour batter into prepared pans.

 3. Pour milk in the cup.

 4. Put some sugar in it.

 5. Mix it properly with a teaspoon.
